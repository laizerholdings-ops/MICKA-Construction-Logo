Our Story & Expertise

At MICKA Construction & Maintenance, we don't just build structures; we build lasting relationships through excellence. Our journey began with a simple goal: to provide the region with reliable, high-standard construction and maintenance services that balance modern innovation with environmental responsibility.

Whether we are laying a new driveway, installing custom drywall, or maintaining industrial facilities, our team approaches every task with precision and passion. We understand that your property is a significant investment. This is why we utilize only the highest quality materials and sustainable building practices to ensure durability, safety, and aesthetic appeal for years to come.

Our versatile team is equipped to handle a wide spectrum of needs—from delicate residential gardening and interior painting to robust general construction and professional fencing solutions. At MICKA, your vision is our mission, and your satisfaction is the true measure of our success.
